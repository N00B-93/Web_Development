About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located at Ocala, Florida. We help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back to the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.



We help many Animals such as:

  • Racoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles


Did you find an Injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
